Nigerian Puff Puffs

Puff puffs are small, round, deep-fried dough balls, popular in Nigerian cuisine. To make them, prepare a batter using flour, sugar, yeast, water, and optional flavorings. Allow the batter to rise, then scoop small portions into hot oil and fry until they puff up and turn golden brown. Puff puffs make for a delightful and slightly sweet snack.
