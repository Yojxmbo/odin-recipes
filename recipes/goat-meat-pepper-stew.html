Nigerian Goat Meat Pepper Stew (with Rice)

Description

Nigerian Goat Meat Pepper soup is a staple in most Nigerian households. You can whip up a big batch anytime for a light, satisfying meal. It is also very popular during parties and gatherings.

Nigerian pepper soup is a very healthy and nutritious soup. If you're feeling down or tired, this African pepper soup has the amazing ability to make you feel better instantly. It also brings tremendous relief when you have a stuffy nose or are congested.

Ingredients

  • Goat Meat
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Pepper Soup Spice
  • Thyme
  • Bouillon
  • Water

Steps

  1. Wash and drain the goat meat. Cut the onions and set aside. Place the goat meat in a pot and add about 6 cups of water. Add the chopped onions, thyme, bouillon, and salt. Boil the meat till it is tender. It will take roughly an hour. Keep an eye on it and make sure all the water does not evaporate and add a bit more as needed.
  2. After about an hour, the water will be well reduced at this point, so add 4 more cups of water. Add the pepper soup spice, cayenne pepper, and crayfish. Taste for salt and add more if needed. Simmer for 20 minutes on medium heat.
  3. Start preparing/cooking/boiling Rice on the side.
  4. It can take a while for the meat to become tender, so you can cook it in an instant pot to save time.
  5. Place all the ingredients in your instant pot and add 8 cups of water. Set your instant pot to High Pressure for 40 minutes. Let the pressure naturally release when it's ready.
  6. Serve yourself with Rice. Enjoy!
